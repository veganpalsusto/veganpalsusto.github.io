Conchas –translates to shells– are the most famous Mexican sweet bread rolls.

We offer our two award winning classic flavours of vegan conchas: cacao and vanilla.

We also offer the Marranitos, a type of pan dulce is reminiscent of gingerbread.

Sometimes we do some limited editions with lovely decorations, they will appear at special occasions, or some holidays. Be on the lookout for these!
